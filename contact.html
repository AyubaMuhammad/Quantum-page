Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start a new project?

We can typically begin new projects within 2-3 weeks of contract signing. For urgent requirements, we offer expedited onboarding that can reduce this timeline to 1 week. The exact timeline depends on project complexity and resource availability.

Do you work with startups and small businesses?

Absolutely! We work with organizations of all sizes, from startups to enterprise-level companies. We offer scalable solutions and flexible engagement models to meet different budget requirements while maintaining our high standards of quality and security.

What industries do you specialize in?

While our technology solutions are applicable across industries, we have extensive experience in financial services, healthcare, e-commerce, agriculture, and manufacturing. Our team includes specialists who understand the unique regulatory and operational requirements of these sectors.

Do you provide ongoing support after implementation?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support packages including 24/7 monitoring, proactive maintenance, performance optimization, and dedicated technical support. Our managed services ensure your technology continues to deliver value long after implementation.

What is your typical project engagement model?

We offer flexible engagement models including fixed-price projects, time-and-materials contracts, and dedicated team arrangements. During our initial consultation, we'll recommend the approach that best fits your project requirements, budget, and timeline.
